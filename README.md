## What is kronos-india?

Kronos-india scans the National Stock Exchange of India for trading opportunities. The software finds stocks with high momentum and identifies patterns for both short and long positions. It combines traditional price analysis with machine learning models to provide data for your trading decisions. This tool automates the process of watching the market so you can track performance and trends throughout the trading day.

## 🚀 Using the Application

Once you finish the installation, you can launch the software using the desktop shortcut. 

### Initial Setup
When you open the application for the first time:
*   The software will check for current market data.
*   You might see a loading screen while the AI models sync with the server.
*   The system will ask for initial settings, such as your preferred alert sensitivity.

### Identifying Trades
The main dashboard serves as your primary view. The application updates every few minutes to scan the Nifty 50 and other NSE indices. 
*   LONG signals suggest buying a stock when the AI detects upward momentum supported by technical indicators. 
*   SHORT signals suggest selling a stock when the system detects downward pressure and weak technical indicators.

### Tracking Performance
Use the Performance tab to see how past signals performed. The application keeps a log of all generated trades. You can view the entry price, the timestamp of the signal, and the current status of the trade. This helps you understand the effectiveness of the model in current market conditions.

## ❓ Common Questions

**Does the software execute trades automatically?**
No. This tool acts as an assistant to provide information. You must place your own trades through your preferred brokerage platform.

**How often does the data update?**
The system pulls index and stock data during market hours. The update frequency depends on your internet connection speed.

**What do I do if the software closes unexpectedly?**
Restart the application. If issues persist, check your internet connection or run the installer again to repair the files.

**Does this software work for non-NSE stocks?**
This version focuses exclusively on the Indian stock market.

## 🛡 Security and Privacy

Your data stays local to your machine. The software connects to our servers only to fetch stock prices and model updates. It never accesses your brokerage accounts or your personal bank information. We do not store your trading history on our servers. Keep your installation files and application logs on your local device to maintain your privacy.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, try these steps:

1.  Restart the application to clear any stuck processes.
2.  Check for Windows updates. The application relies on modern Windows libraries to run the AI components.
3.  Ensure your firewall allows the application to access the internet. It needs this access to receive real-time market data.
4.  If the dashboard remains empty, wait five minutes. The data takes time to load during the first launch of the day.

For further assistance, revisit the repository documentation. You can also monitor the issues section on the main page to see if other users report similar behaviors.
